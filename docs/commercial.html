Commercial

Commercial

We specialize in helping small businesses who want to open a new business or expand and reconfigure an existing one to successfully maneuver the design and regulatory process in Alexandria, Virginia.

We do this by using proven design management tools and advanced 3D virtual reality technology to help you to better understand your design decisions.

We believe that the same skills developed in our residential practice are directly transferable to our commercial work. At our core we believe that a successful design will produce a healthy, well functioning environment and a better life for all. We achieve this through smart planning and a specialization in retail and light commercial design. We know that a well designed space makes people feel good.

When Starbucks expanded to the east coast, they selected Wheeler Architects to assist them. We successfully opened their first 23 stores throughout the Washington metropolitan area.

When a client in their newly-renovated business said "we knew it was going to be lovely, the model showed us that, but we never realized exactly how much better it would function".

If you have questions on what to look for in an architect for your project or how we work together feel free to contact me or download one of our helpful guides.

I look forward to collaborating with you on your future.

Site Selection Review Services

Are you opening a business for the first time? Are you in the market to lease or buy a property for your companies future home? Is your business doing so well that it is necessary to relocate or expand?

Consult the experts before you commit your time and money.

Don’t wait until after you have made your expensive investment to find out that you have made a mistake and there are restrictions or unknown conditions that will limit what you can do with your property or generate unanticipated costs. We see too often businesses who lease or purchase a property with the intent of moving, expanding or opening the new business, without fully understanding the restrictions or costs involved with such an endeavor.

This can lead to unexpected cost overruns, time delays, disruptions to your business, and/or compromises in creating or relocating your business. The Washington metropolitan area is a heavily regulated environment with many potential pitfalls for the unwary. Our Site Selection Review service is a valuable first step that will help you establish a clear vision for the way forward.
