McLean Sunroom Addition

Whole House Renovation and Addition
The Challenge:

The issue facing the owners was there was no way to take advantage of the lovely wooded backyard and lower level pool.

The Solution:

What was really needed was a transitional space from the kitchen to the exterior. We accomplished this by the addition of a multi-purpose sunroom and expansion of the small deck to a much more spacious one. The new sunrooms indirect lighting, skylights and large windows create a bright, vibrant room. Adding a new stair to the lower level in-ground pool tied all the exterior functions together to create a wonderful outdoor entertaining area. New landscape lighting illuminates the deck, garden and mature trees.
