Rosemont Whole Home Addition and Renovation

Whole House Renovation and Addition
The Challenge:

An estate sale precipitated the renovation and addition to this early original 20th century home on a large corner lot. Never having been updated the home was functionally obsolete and in need of all new utilities and services.

The Solution:

Sited to the south side of the site, a large addition doubled the size of the home. Setting the addition back from the existing home made it possible to minimize the bulk and scale of the addition and have it harmonize with the existing massing.

Partially filling in the front porch allowed for a new covered entry and a more generous living room and dining room. The addition's new kitchen and attached family room is an ideal gathering space providing smooth flow for entertaining family and friends. And the south facing covered porch offers outdoor dining and a place for a glass of wine before dinner. On the second floor a new sky-lit gallery for the children opens to bedrooms, baths and the master bedroom and bath en-suite with enchanting cathedral ceiling.
