About

I am Michael Wheeler, Principal Architect of Wheeler Architects in Alexandria, Virginia.

I have been helping clients improve their homes and businesses for more than 35 years. During this time I have learned many methods to help my clients realize their dreams.

Our practice specializes in residential and small business design, using our proven management systems to focus your dreams, create realizable goals and complete functional and attractive projects. Our goal is provide you a remarkable experience throughout the process and to design a space that will bring you and your family or clients delight every day you are there.

Wheeler Architects does not focus on a single style of architecture. Our signature is in our thoughtful, customized approach, which begins and ends with our clientsâ€™ needs and desires. When you work with us, we partner with you every step of the way.

You will occupy your home or business for many years to come, and our job is to create an environment that will give you the comfort and functionality you need. We do not bring preconceived ideas to the table or try to impose our opinions on you.

Each of our projects is different, because each of our clients is different.

By combining program, function, form and budget we work together to design the perfect living space to meet your unique needs.

We believe in giving back to the community, as a long time resident of Alexandria Mr. Wheeler has served the community in many capacities. His 12 years on the Old and Historic District Board of Architectural Review informed his view of design. Reviewing several thousand residential and commercial projects improved his design sense. As a current member of the Building Code Board of Appeals we are involved in and have a strong understanding and relationship with Code Enforcement.

We would love to speak with you about your ideal project. Contact us today to get the conversation going.
